Vegan Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

This cuisine is a delicious one originated from India. It's recipe is mind-blowing.

Yummy vegan curry dish. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Cook: 20 mins
Total: 30 mins
Prep: 10 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 6 servings. Nutritional Info

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 teaspoons of minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (drained)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 sweet potato (cubed)
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chile flakes
  • 1 cup baby spinach

Directions

Step 1

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger until softened about 5 minutes
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato
  4. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until tender about 15 minutes

Step 2

  1. Season with garam masala, cummin, turmeric, chile flakes and salt
  2. Add spinach right before serving
